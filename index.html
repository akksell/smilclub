What is SMIL.?

smil. translates to “smile in english” from norwegian. Why norwegian? Why not? Based out of Texas, smil. hopes to spread positivity and joy through its minimalistic approach to design and apparel. While the brand only reaches the hat market, it hopes to grow and put its reputable mark on other types of apparel in the near future. smil. is always looking to expand its reach and currently has plans to establish itself in other cities across Texas.

Texas A&M University in College Station is at the heart of this expansion project. The main goal for this venture is to promote the brand to students and further bolster its reputation. How will this be done? By continuing its affordable, durable, and friendly design process. Our next release is a limited drop of hats of varying colors coming this fall.
